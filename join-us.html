LawGlitch Partnership Policies

1. Professional Standards: All professionals must maintain the highest ethical standards and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

2. Service Fees: LawGlitch charges a 15% commission on consultation fees and a 10% commission on full case representation.

3. Client Communication: All client communications must be conducted through LawGlitch platform for the first 30 days.

4. Availability: Professionals must maintain accurate availability calendars and respond to client inquiries within 24 hours.

5. Background Checks: LawGlitch reserves the right to verify credentials and perform background checks on all professionals.

6. Termination: Either party may terminate the partnership with 30 days notice. LawGlitch may terminate immediately for violations of these terms.

7. Data Protection: Professionals must protect client confidentiality and comply with all data protection regulations.
